Basic questions:

Why did we choose homeostasis and body systems?

Homeostasis is extremely important to everything that goes on in your body. It also ties in closely with body systems. Without either of them, we wouldn't function properly.

Do I really need to know this?

It benefits to learn how homeostasis and body systems are and what they do. Even if you aren't going into the medical industry, you could still use this knowledge to help understand why you might be sick or what's happening to your body. Not to mention that more knowledge can't hurt.

Where does this happen?

You can find homeostasis and body systems working everywhere. Take any part of the body, and you'll, with almost 100% certainty, find these systems working to keep you alive and working properly.
