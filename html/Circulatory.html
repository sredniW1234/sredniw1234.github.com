Quick Overview of the Circulatory System:

* This is somewhat of a TLDR * The circulatory system is the system in which blood is pumped around the body delivering vital resources to cells.

1.  What is it?

The circulatory is an essential part of the body which regulates all the blood and where the blood goes. Without it, you would cease to be alive because there would be no blood pumping throughout your body
